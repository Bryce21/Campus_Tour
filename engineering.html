Engineering at Awesome University

Types of Engineering

  • Here at Awesome University, we offer many different types of Engineering degrees to give students the chance to find the best career path. We offer Petroleum Engineering, Nuclear Engineering, Mining Engineering, Metallurgical/Materials Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Industrial/Manufacturing/Systems Engineering, Engineering Science and Physics, Engineering Management, General Engineering, Electrical, Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, Civil Engineering, Chemical Engineering, Biomedical Engineering, Biological/Agricultural Engineering, Architectural Engineering, and Aerospace Engineering.

    Engineering Courses

  • As there are many different Engineering degrees, here is a sample of the courses for Electrical Engineering: Calculus I and II, Economics, Physics I and II, Intro to EE, Logic, Mechatronics, Alternative Energy, Fiber, Wireless, Photonics, Solid State, Digital Communications Systems, Probability and Statistics, Computer Architecture, Engineering Math, Databases, and Integrated System Design.

    Engineering Club

  • If you are interested in hands-on Engineering outside the classroom, then the Engineering Club is right for you. The Engineering Club allows students to share knowledge of different fields of science that connect to Engineering, offer hands-on learning, create projects, educate students on possible careers, and foster discussion and learning for the field. The club has hosted various challenges to create projects. Welding with Chocolate challenges participants to build structures to demonstrate strength, welding, and melting testing. Wind Power challenges students to build a wind powered machine that can lift a cup off the floor. The Sounds Good Challenge demonstrates the nature of sound. See below to participate in any of these events.
