Awesome University

Here at AU if you plan to go into the STEM field you will find a diverse array of learning, social/cultural, professional, and practice communities in the College of Sciences. These communities and your fellow students will provide you a supportive climate and a culture of belonging where you are respected and affirmed. In addition to our academic programs, the AU College of Sciences includes an alliance of nationally prominent centers, institutes, and affiliated research and outreach units working to fulfill our mission: Making Educational and Systems Work for All.
