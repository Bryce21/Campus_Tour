Architecture at Awesome University

Why Should You Study Architecture?

  • Study interesting subjects like Structural Design, Environmental Systems, Architecture History and Theory, Contemporary Spatial Practices, and Architecture Methods and Strategies. Pursue exciting careers as an Architect, Architectural and Engineering Manager, Urban and Regional Planner, and Landscape Architect. Develop new skills such as creativity, technical skills, visualization, analysis, and communication.

    Design Buildings

  • Students learn how to create buildings for residential and urban areas using basic computer-aided design software and freehand drawing. Some programs offer concentrations such as history of architecture or architectural technology, and there may be dual architecture degrees in areas like business administration, civil engineering and regional planning.

    Architecture Club

  • The Architecture Club is a student-lead organization that focuses on issues and events related to architecture. The goal is to diversify design students' lifestyle through design workshops, city-walks, volunteering and travel opportunities, competitions, and networking events. Details can be found below.
